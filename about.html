About Waikanae Beach Takeaways

Your local taste of authentic Kiwi fish & chips

Fresh Food, Coastal Views

Located just steps from the beautiful Waikanae Beach, we've been serving the local community and visitors with premium fish & chips and classic Kiwi takeaways.

Our commitment to quality means we use only the freshest local fish, hand-cut chips, and time-tested recipes that have made us a favorite destination on the Kapiti Coast.

What Makes Us Special

  • Fresh Local Fish: Sourced daily from local suppliers for the best taste and quality
  • Hand-Cut Chips: Made from premium potatoes, cut and cooked fresh daily
  • Homemade Specialties: Our meat patties and fritters are made in-house
  • Family Friendly: Special kids packs and family deals for everyone
  • Coastal Location: Perfect spot to grab takeaways before a beach walk

Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Waikanae Beach, our takeaway shop has become a beloved local institution. We pride ourselves on maintaining traditional Kiwi fish & chip shop values while serving up consistently delicious food that keeps families coming back generation after generation.

Whether you're a local regular or a visitor exploring the stunning Kapiti Coast, we welcome you with the warm hospitality that New Zealand is famous for. Our team is passionate about food and committed to making every meal special.

Perfect Beachside Location

Waikanae Beach is one of the Kapiti Coast's most beautiful spots, and we're proud to call it home. After picking up your fish & chips from us, you can:

  • Take a relaxing walk along the pristine beach
  • Enjoy your meal with stunning ocean views
  • Watch the sunset over the Tasman Sea
  • Let the kids play safely on the family-friendly beach
  • Explore the nearby Waikanae River mouth
